Africanized Honey Bees (Killer Bees)

Africanized Honey Bees, commonly known as Killer Bees, are a hybrid species that resulted from the interbreeding of African honey bees with European honey bees. They are known for their aggressive behavior and can be a threat to humans and animals.

Prevention:

  • Be Aware: Stay informed about the presence of Africanized Honey Bees in your area.
  • Protective Clothing: Wear protective clothing, such as beekeeping suits, when working in areas with potential bee activity.
  • Seal Entry Points: Seal any openings in buildings where bees could establish nests.
